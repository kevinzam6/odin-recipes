Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

Description

This pasta recipe will help you make a deliciously creamy Alfredo pasta with the best sauce. It uses fresh cream and parmesan cheese to make the Alfredo sauce in just a few minutes! You can then toss your favorite noodles into the sauce to create the ultimate comfort food that will have you coming back for more!

Ingredients List (3 Servings)

  • 226 grams fettuccine noodles - 1/2 pound
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 to 1.5 teaspoons salt or add as required
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons Butter - 30 grams, unsalted or salted
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 6 tablespoons vegetarian parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
